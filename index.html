our membership fees

You are welcome to join our community free of charge. You will have access to our content and software free of charge. And you can earn credits for your contributions of time to community goals, and those credits can be redeemed for support from the community.

As we produce sellable software, that income will be divvyed up amongst the contributors based on a formula of contribution that we collectively decide. Until that point we offer a variety of microfunding options for members and clients alike.
